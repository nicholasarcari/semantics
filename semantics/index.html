False Pocket Band Website

Hello hello,

This is website dedicated to the variety of content associated with the band False Pocket. As a fairly new band, the group has had no home to store any of its material.

Material includes: A biography of the band Contact information Photos Videos Studio recordings Links to music Merchandise Upcoming shows

The design of the website will have a simple look, emulating the style and attitude of the members of the band and its music.

With the addition of a website, False Pocket will be able to market themselves in a fashion designed for easy public access. The assumption is that this will help spread the word that False Pocket is a serious group, committed to their music, and dedicated to their fans.
